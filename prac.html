Demonstrating Positions

This section is here to create enough content to make the page scrollable. As you scroll down, notice a few things:

The header at the very top has position: sticky. It scrolls with the content until it hits the top of the viewport, where it "sticks" in place.

The profile card above has position: relative. By itself, this doesnâ€™t do much, but it creates a positioning context for its child elements.

The notification badge has position: absolute, which positions it relative to the boundaries of the profile card, its nearest "positioned" ancestor.

Meanwhile, this section of text is using the default position: static, meaning it just flows naturally as part of the page layout.
